#### INFO Useful (items, spells, dice)

"Useful dice rolls" (etc.) are used for rolls that solve a problem in the story or overcome an obstacle. In combat, they can only be used in exceptional cases or certain maneuvers. The [[LOREGENT/1 - ROLEPLAY/05 COMBAT/RPG - Victory levels\|combat result]] in such a case must be pacifistic.
